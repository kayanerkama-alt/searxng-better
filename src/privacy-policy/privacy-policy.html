🔐 ATOMIC SEARCH PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is our atomic priority. This policy explains how we protect your data.

1. 🔒 Zero Logging Policy

We NEVER log your search queries, user agent information, or your unmasked IP address. Search requests are processed and immediately discarded - no traces left behind.

We use the built-in SearXNG rate limiter, which keeps a SHA256 hash of a salted IP address for a maximum of 10 minutes, solely for operational purposes. You can review the rate limiter source on GitHub.

2. 🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

We use minimal cookies only to save your preferences (theme, language, etc.). These cookies are never used for tracking and we never use third-party cookies.

Your saved preferences are visible in Preferences. You can clear all preferences at any time.

3. 🛡️ Security & TLS

We always handle TLS termination ourselves and never use third-party services like Cloudflare, AWS CloudFront, or Lambda. Your search queries are encrypted end-to-end from your browser to our servers.

We utilize our own global server network to minimize exposure points and provide the best possible privacy protection.

4. 🌐 Search Engine Privacy

When you search through Atomic Search, your query is distributed to multiple search engines simultaneously. This means:

• No single engine sees your full search history
• Queries are not linked to your identity
• Optional image proxy prevents direct contact between you and image hosts

5. 🚫 What We DON'T Do

• We don't create user profiles
• We don't sell or share your data
• We don't track you across the web
• We don't log browsing patterns
• We don't use analytics that track users

6. 🔧 Data Portability & Control

You have full control over your data:

• View and clear your preferences anytime in Preferences
• Use private browsing mode without cookies
• Export your settings for backup

7. 📝 Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be prominently posted on this page. We recommend checking for updates regularly.

8. 📧 Contact Us

Questions about data protection? Contact us at vojk.au

9. 📅 Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of 2024-10-18.
