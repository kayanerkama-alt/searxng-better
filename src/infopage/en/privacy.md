# Privacy Policy

**Atomic Search** is designed with privacy as the core principle.

## What We DON'T Do

- ❌ We don't store cookies
- ❌ We don't log your searches
- ❌ We don't track you across the web
- ❌ We don't share your data with anyone
- ❌ We don't create user profiles

## Privacy Features

### 🔒 Zero Tracking
Your searches are never recorded. Every query is processed anonymously.

### 🌐 Tracker Blocking
We automatically block 500+ known trackers, analytics scripts, and fingerprinting attempts.

### 🔗 URL Cleaning
UTM parameters, Google tracking codes, and other tracking tokens are automatically removed from URLs.

### 🚪 Referrer Hiding
Websites won't know where you came from when you click links.

### 🌐 Anonymous Proxy
Optional anonymous routing hides your IP address from search engines.

### 🔒 Encrypted Search
Optional encrypted queries prevent ISP monitoring of your searches.

## Data Retention

- **No logs**: Search queries are never stored
- **No analytics**: We don't use analytics on our service
- **Session isolation**: Each search is processed independently
- **No personal data**: We don't collect names, emails, or any identifying information

## Your Control

You can:
- Use the search engine without accepting any cookies
- Clear all local data anytime
- Use our privacy features without registration
- Export or delete your preferences anytime
